Bank account kata
=================

Think of your personal bank account experience
When in doubt, go for the simplest solution

Requirements
------------

Deposit and Withdrawal
Transfer
Account statement (date, amount, balance)
Statement printing
Statement filters (just deposits, withdrawal, date)
